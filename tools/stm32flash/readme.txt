Firmware flashing tool for DS203

Downloaded from 
https://sourceforge.net/p/stm32flash/wiki/Home/

FW images provided by JPA
original FW and FW without license verification included

on connector CN7 connect pin 2 (BT0) to pin1 (VCC), and use USB to UART converter connected to pins 4 (RX), 5 (TX) and 6 (GND)
